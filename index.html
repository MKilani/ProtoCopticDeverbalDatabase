About This Database

This database presents a comprehensive collection of Coptic forms, their grammatical features, and interpretations. The data is primarily drawn from Crum's lexicon and Kilani (2026) studies. Users can explore the table, sort columns by any feature, and search for specific forms or roots. It aims to support linguistic research and comparative studies in Coptic morphology and semantics.

Column Descriptions & Abbreviations

Dialect: Coptic dialect (Sahidic, Bohairic, etc.)
Coptic Form: The form as written in Coptic script
Coptic Transcription: Latin transliteration of the Coptic form
Number, Gender: Grammatical number and gender
Coptic Meaning (Crum): Meaning according to Crum's lexicon
Class, Subclass, Property Type: Semantic/grammatical classification
Literal Interpretation (Kilani 2026), Vocalic Pattern, Interpretation (Kilani 2026): Explanatory notes
Suffix, Distinctive Vowel, Root ID, Pre-Coptic Root, Root Type: Morphological information
TLA IS, TLA en, TLE de, TLA - date first attestation: Cross-linguistic references and attestation date
